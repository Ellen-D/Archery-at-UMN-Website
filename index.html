Welcome!

The UMN archery club is a student group that provides an outlet or introduction to archery with a focus on indoor education. All experience levels are welcome. We look forward to meeting you!

FAQ

  • Do I need my own equipment?
    We provide equipment and instruction because many of the people that join are new.
  • What if I have my own equipment?
    If you have your own equipment, you may use it as long as the draw weight is less than 65lbs. We can't store your bow for you because there is no room but hope to provide this service in the future.
  • Do I need to attend every practice?
    Nope! However, you're encouraged to attend weekly to improve your skills.
  • Where do I look for meeting cancellations or date changes?
    Check our Facebook group for the fastest updates, or we will update here under "When and Where."
